stryf-issues
============

This is the public issue repository for https://styf.io

If you have an issue you'd like to report, or an feature you would like to suggest, please feel free to add it in the issues section.

Thanks!

## Reporting an Issue

Its quick and easy! Just click over to the issues tab and press "New Issue". Please describe the problem as best you can. Someone from the team will follow up with you to make sure your bug gets addressed!

## Requesting a Feature

You can request a feature by clicking over to the issues tab and pressing "New Issue". Please describe the feature you would like in as much detail as possible. Someone from the team will follow up with you about your feature. We love your ideas and want to hear them!

--Stryf Team
